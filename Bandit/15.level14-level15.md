<hr/>

<b>Solution</b><br/>

<p>
<ol>

<code> nc <target-ip> <target-port> </code>

<li> "localhost" or "127.0.0.1" both can be entered in place of "<target-ip>" </li>

<code> nc localhost 30000 </code>
<code> nc localhost 30000 </code>
